Sloppy Joes

What Is a Sloppy Joes?

A Sloppy Joes is a sandwich consisting of ground beef and onions in a tomato-based sauce served on a hamburger bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup ketchup, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns, split

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir lean ground beef in the hot skillet until some of the fat starts to render, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Add onion and bell pepper; continue to cook until vegetables have softened and beef is cooked through, 3 to 5 more minutes.
  4. Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and garlic powder; season with salt and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes.
  6. Divide meat mixture evenly among hamburger buns.
